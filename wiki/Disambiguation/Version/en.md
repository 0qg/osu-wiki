# Version

Version can have four meanings:

- The specific [beatmap](/wiki/Beatmaps) of a [beatmapset](/wiki/Beatmapsets)
  - This term be used in place of "beatmap" to prevent disambiguation
- The version of a [skin](/wiki/Skinning)
- The release date of the osu! client
- The build release of the osu! client (e.g., `Stable`, `Stable (Fallback)`, `Beta`, `Cutting Edge`.)

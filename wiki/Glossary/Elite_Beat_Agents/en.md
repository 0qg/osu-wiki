# Elite Beat Agents

*See also: [osu! Tatake! Ouendan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Osu!_Tatakae!_Ouendan "Wikipedia")* <!-- this needs an stub to link to -->

**Elite Beat Agents** (*EBA*) refers to the original video game [Elite Beat Agents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elite_Beat_Agents), developed by [iNiS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/INiS) and released for the [Nintendo DS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_DS) in 2006. It is one of the original [DS games](/wiki/Glossary/DS_Games) that the [osu!standard](/wiki/Game_Modes/osu!) [game mode](/wiki/Game_Modes) is based on.

# Elite Beat Agents

**Elite Beat Agents** or *EBA* refers to the game called [Elite Beat Agents](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elite_Beat_Agents) developed by [iNiS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/INiS) and released for the [Nintendo DS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_DS) in 2006.

EBA is one of the [DS games](/wiki/Glossary/DS_Games) that the [osu!standard](/wiki/Game_Modes/osu!) gamemode is based on.

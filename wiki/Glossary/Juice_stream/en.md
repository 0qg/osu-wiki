# Juice stream

**Juice streams** are an element element in [osu!catch](/wiki/Game_Modes/osu!catch) which includes both Drops and Droplets. Drops give a score of 100, equivalent to slider ticks in [osu!standard](/wiki/Game_Modes/osu!), while Droplets give a score of 10, equivalent to the slider tick in osu!standard.

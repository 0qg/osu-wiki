# Mapping and modding timeline

Mapping and modding systems are constantly improving. The **Mapping and modding timeline** aims to document the biggest system changes and to serve as a reference tool when incorporating new changes.

## 2007

### September

- **2007-09-17:** osu! is publicly released.

### October

- **2007-10-21:** [Ranking Criteria](/wiki/Ranking_Criteria) launches. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/439 -->
- **2007-10-29:**: Forum thread icons are standardized for the ranking process. A `star` (![star icon](/wiki/shared/icon/star.gif)) marks good maps that need more work, while a `bubble` (![bubble icon](/wiki/shared/icon/bubble.gif)) marks beatmaps considered for ranking. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/t/619 + missing info on flame/nuke -->

**200 beatmaps** were ranked in 2007.

<!-- missing formation of the BAT -->

## 2008

### February

- **2008-02-04:** [Ivalset](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/827) is promoted as the first [BAT manager](/wiki/Modding/Bat_Managers). <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/36869?start=81784 + https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/9639 -->

### March

- **2008-03-11:** [Graveyard](/wiki/Beatmaps#graveyard) beatmap category is implemented to house inactive beatmaps. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/15290 -->

### July

- **2008-07-31:** Approved <!-- TODO: stub --> beatmap category is implemented to provide leaderboards to beatmaps inappropriate for ranking. Common reasons for approval initially included length above 5 minutes and scores above 20 million. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/4936 -->

### December

- **2008-12-20:** Requirements for joining the [Beatmap Appreciation Team (*BAT*)](/wiki/Modding/Beatmap_Appreciation_Team) are published. Members are hand-picked based on criteria outlined in the [announcement's forum post](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/8416).

**690 beatmaps** were ranked in 2008.

## 2009

### March

- **2009-03-04:** The first Delegation of the BADASS is held, <!-- TODO: stub --> allowing current BAT vote on potential new members. Prior to this, BAT were added based on an informal vote from osu!'s `#bat` [IRC](/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat) channel. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/10638 -->

### November

- **2009-11-10:** [Pasonia](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/43345) is appointed as BAT manager after [Ivalset](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/827) steps down. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/19833 -->

### December

- **2009-12-27:** The [Mapping Assistance Team (*MAT*)](/wiki/Modding/Mapping_Assistance_Team) launched with [Ephemeral](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/102335) as the [MAT Leader](/wiki/Modding/MAT_Leaders). MAT members can mark beatmaps with [proto-bubbles](/wiki/Modding/Proto-bubble) which indicate quality beatmaps that BAT members should review.<!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/21836 + https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/21854 -->

**1400 beatmaps** were ranked in 2009.

## 2010

### July

- **2010-07-24:** The first iteration of the Community Mentorship program <!-- TODO: stub --> is launched, allowing users to learn about mapping and modding directly from volunteer MAT and BAT members. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/34111 + https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/34194 -->

### August

- **2010-08-04:** MAT are granted permission to nominate beatmaps for ranking with normal `bubble` icons, as opposed to proto-bubbles. This gave MAT considerably more influence in the ranking process.<!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/38403 -->
- **2010-08-27:** [ztrot](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/6347) is appointed as MAT leader after [Ephemeral](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/102335) steps down. Leadership is tentatively passed between the two users until ztrot holds control in February 2011.

### December

- **2010-12-31:** [Derekku](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/91341) is appointed as BAT leader after [Pasonia](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/43345) steps down. <!-- missing source -->

**1864 beatmaps** were ranked in 2010.

## 2011

### March

- **2011-03-09:** MAT members are granted permission to  nominate beatmaps for approval with `bubble` icons. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/48108 -->

### August

- **2011-08-11:** MAT members are no longer allowed to nominate or vote on new additions to their team. The BAT now have full responsibility for MAT additions. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/38806 -->

### October

- **2011-10-08:** [Ranking Criteria subforum](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/87) opened for public discussion on [Ranking Criteria](/wiki/Ranking_Criteria) proposals. These discussions were previously held by BAT members in a private subforum. <!-- date is not exact. the earliest thread in the subforum was posted on 2011-10-08 -->

**1368 beatmaps** were ranked in 2011.

## 2012

### July

- **2012-07-22:** The first iteration of [Performance Points](/wiki/Performance_Points) known as [ppv1](/wiki/Performance_Points/ppv1) was established, replacing score ranking. <!-- TODO: stub -->

### August

- **2012-08-21:** Approved beatmaps reward Ranked score. <!-- changelog -->

**1460 beatmaps** were ranked in 2012.

## 2013

### March

- **2013-03-20:** The BAT is split into the BAT and GMT, clarifying the roles of modding and moderating members. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/123465 -->

### April

- **2013-04-23:** The MAT is merged with the BAT. All members can bubble and rank beatmaps. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/129176 -->

### May

- **2013-05-15:** The [Triumvir Conglomerate](/wiki/BAT_Managers#triumvir-conglomerate) is formed, splitting the role of BAT manager between 3 users from different regions in an attempt to promote equal input among mapping subgroups. [Garven](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/244216) represented North America, [Nastume Rin](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/151679) represented Asia, and [Kurai](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/77089) represented Europe.<!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/132665 -->

### August

- **2013-08-01:** Beatmap unranking policy is implemented, allowing BAT members to unrank a beatmap within 1 week of ranking. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/146533 -->
- **2013-08-02:** The first osu!taiko BAT were added, signifying a separation of game mode permissions in the group. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/83704?start=2474091 -->

### December

- **2013-12-12:** Beatmaps begin promotion to the [Qualified](/wiki/Beatmaps#qualified) category, replacing the option of unranking a beatmap within 1 week. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/171257 -->
- **2013-12-22:** The [Triumvir Conglomerate](/wiki/BAT_Managers#triumvir-conglomerate) cycles its regional leaders. [Charles445](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/85000) represents North America, [popner](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/759860) represents Asia, and [Loctav](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/71366) represents Europe. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/172289 -->

**1327 beatmaps** were ranked in 2013.

## 2014

### February

- **2014-02-10:** Mapping and modding system internal managmenet is shifted from the forums to Trello. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/185379 -->
- **2014-02-18:** Approved beatmap category is retired. Marathon beatmaps are promoted to Ranked with 3 BAT nominations (2 bubbles + 1 flame). <!-- date is not accurate. this is date of last approved map's approval -->

<!-- some time around here p3n becomes the equivilent of BATmanager but there is no documentation. probably on Trello -->

### August

- **2014-08-21:** The Quality Assurance Team (*QAT*) <!-- TODO: stub --> splits off from the current BAT. The BAT (often called newBAT at this time) nominated beatmaps for rank, while the QAT ensured those beatmaps were sufficient quality. This essentially reverses the merging of the BAT and MAT last year. The split was implemented as part of a manual trial for [moddingv2](/wiki/Beatmap_Discussion) attempting to automate BAT promotion and retirement. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2014-08-21-restructuring-of-the-bat -->

### October

- **2014-10-15:** The [Beatmap Management subforum](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/115) was created to replace Trello and increase public accessibility to the BAT. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/258489 -->

### November

- **2014-11-14:** New BAT promotion was held via public votes among current BAT/QAT. Users with 15 or more votes and automatically calculated modding activity would be promoted to the group intermittently. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/258555 -->

**1381 beatmaps** were ranked in 2014.

## 2015

### February

- **2015-02-01:** Red usernames are removed from BAT and BAT is renamed to Beatmap Appreciators. After receiving suggestions from the public, the Beatmap Appreciators are quickly renamed to [Beatmap Nominators (*BN*)](/wiki/People/The_Team/Beatmap_Nominators/). <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/292443 -->
- **2015-02-24:** New BAT applications replace BAT voting. The only criteria was an applicant's automatically calculated modding activity.

### June

- **2015-06-04:** Realtime Beatmap Nominator Ranking is introduced to automatically assess BN nomination quality/activity. This system is dropped soon after. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/334994 -->
- **2015-06-06:** QAT disqualifications are anonymized on the forums in response to attacks on the QAT. All posts were marked as written by a user called "Quality Assurance Team" and referred to by the community as the "QAT bot". <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/search.php?author_id=6616586&sr=posts -->

### August

- **2015-08-25:** Qualified beatmaps no longer reward players with [Performance Points](/wiki/Performance_Points), alleviating tension between players and the QAT. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/352460 -->
- **2015-08-26:** Anonymous QAT disqualifications are discontinuied. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/search.php?author_id=6616586&sr=posts -->

### December

- **2015-12-27:** BN tests are implemented for BN applications. These tests required users to answer a multiple choice form and mod a problem-filled beatmap. <!-- date used is when the "BN Examinations - Answer Keys" document was created. first test was published a month or so later -->

**1934 beatmaps** were ranked in 2015.

## 2016

### February

- **2016-02-13:** Ranking criteria council <!-- TODO: stub --> is formed to reform the [Ranking Criteria](/wiki/Ranking_Criteria). Public proposals on the Ranking Criteira subforum are no longer considered. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/420229 -->
- **2016-02-24:** Mapset and spread restructure is introduced to limit the number of difficulties in each difficulty tear per beatmap set. Negative public reception caused this to be rolled back immediately and its [forum thread](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/420223) accumulated 614 responses in 24 hours, making it osu!'s fastest moving thread. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/420223 -->

### April

- **2016-04-25:** Excluding song metadata, the QAT begins checking qualified maps for issues reactivitly rather than proactively. Users reported qualified beatmaps to the QAT for disqualification. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/447417 -->

### August

- **2016-08-05:** The [Quality Assurance Team blog](/wiki/Quality_Assurance_Team_Blog) is created to increase transparency for QAT decision making and community activity. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/578437 -->
- **2016-08-26:** The [Code of Conduct](/wiki/Rules/Code_of_Conduct_for_Modding_and_Mapping) is implemented to establish modding behavior expectations. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/447557 -->

### September

- **2016-09-30:** The Community Mentorship Program launches under new management and with a fully redesigned format centered around [Discord](https://discord.com/new) communication.

### October

- **2016-10-17:** [Project Loved](/wiki/Project_Loved) is created to give leaderboards to popular maps that cannot be ranked for various reasons. Beatmaps are promoted to the [Loved](/wiki/Beatmaps#loved) category by community vote.

### November

- **2016-11-01:** The beatmap veto system is implemented, allowing BN members to halt a beatmap in the ranking process for subjective reasons. Vetoes could be overwritten by the nominations of Beatmap Nominators previously uninvolved with the beatmap. To allow adequate time to veto, consecutive nominations could not be set within 24 hours of each other. <!-- date is not exact at all, but logs are from deleted discord chat -->
- **2016-11-02:** Beatmap Nominator files are created to keep tabs on all individual BN members. These files included modding/nomination activity, disqualifications, and behavior issues. Members of the BN with 3 or more "strikes" logged in these files would be removed from the group.

**1720 beatmaps** were ranked in 2016.

## 2017

### April

- **2017-04-06:** Beatmap Nominator Tiers <!-- TODO: stub --> are implemented, splitting the BN into two groups. Tier 1 BN could bubble maps, while tier 2 BN could bubble and qualify maps.

### May

- **2017-05-22:** Beatmap Nominator Discord server is created for BN subdivisions, which were focus groups of randomly selected nominators from both tiers led by a member of the QAT.
- **2017-05-23:** Aiess <!-- TODO: stub? --> is created by [Naxess](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/8129817) to provide a feed of mapping related events in osu! Discord servers.

### June

- **2017-06-02:** Marathon beatmaps require 2 nominations instead of 3. This also phases out the use of the `flame` ![flame icon](/wiki/shared/icon/flame.gif) icon. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/631077?start=6050796 -->

### September

- **2017-09-10:** BN tiers are replaced with a [Probationary Beatmap Nominators](/wiki/People/The_Team/Beatmap_Nominators#probationary-beatmap-nominators). Under this system, two probationary BN members could not nominate the same beatmap and users could not be in Probation for more than two months at a time. This change was coined as the "QAT Upheaval" and also resulted in [Okoratu](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/1623405) and [JBHyperion](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4879508) being appointed as QAT leaders. <!-- TODO: stub for QAT Leaders --><!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/640679 -->

### October

- **2017-10-01:** BN tests are replaced by the QAT reviewing each BN applicant's anonymized mods. <!-- date not exact, based on discord logs -->

### December

- **2017-12-17:** [Loctav](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/71366) steps down from involvement in the osu!team, including his heavy participation in the QAT. <!-- https://clips.twitch.tv/BrightFragilePepperoniAllenHuhu -->

**1847 beatmaps** were ranked in 2017.

## 2018

### February

- **2018-02-08:** The [Mappers' Guild](/wiki/Mappers_Guild) is opened to public applications for widespread [Featured Artist](/wiki/Featured_Artists) beatmap creation. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/700654 -->

### March

- **2018-03-01:** QAT separates its tasks into branches: BN evaluation, disqualifications, metadata, spotlights, blog, ranking criteria, and general leadership. <!-- source is discord logs -->
- **2018-03-03:** `#modding` channel is created in the [osu!dev Discord server](/wiki/osu!dev_Discord_server) to shift mapping discussion away from the BN Discord server.
- **2018-03-24:** Ranking criteira subforum is re-opened for public proposal discussions.

### April

- **2018-04-01:** The QAT evaluate BN applications by reviewing non-anonymous mod reviews, as opposed to the previous anonymous mod reviews. <!-- source is discord logs -->
- **2018-04-22:** The QAT holds its first meeting on the osu!dev server in an attempt to increase transparency of QAT affairs and migrate internal discussions away from the BN Discord server. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/735472 -->

### May

- **2018-05-22:** [Mao](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2204515) becomes a QAT leader after [Okoratu](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/1623405) steps down.

### June

- **2018-06-21:** The [Quality Assurance Helper](/wiki/People/The_Team/Beatmap_Nominators/General_Information#quality-assurance-helpers) initiative is created to ensure all qualified beatmaps are reviewed by at least 2 BN or QAT before rank. The program was managed via Trello. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/764134-->

### July

- **2018-07-29:** New beatmap spread rules are implemented, completely reshaping how many difficulties are created in ranked mapsets going forward. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/726474 -->

### December

- **2018-12-12:** The osu!team takes a [negative stance on beatmap commissions](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/840838), acknolwedging that they will happen, but pledging to take no action unless members of the BN or QAT are involved.
- **2018-12-17:** [-Mo-](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2202163) becomes a QAT leader after [JBHyperion](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/4879508) steps down.
- **2018-12-17:** The QAT is declared as having no future in its current state and ideas for reform are encouraged. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/842601 -->

**2749 beatmaps** were ranked in 2018.

## 2019

### January

- **2019-01-29:** A proposal for QAT reform is created as a conglomeration of other users' proposals. This introduces the concept of the [Nomination Assessment Team (*NAT*)](/wiki/People/The_Team/Nomination_Assessment_Team). <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/862002 -->

### March

- **2019-03-01:** The [Mappers' Guild](/wiki/Mappers_Guild) transitions from application-based entry to automatic entry for anyone with 3 ranked maps. [Featured Artist](/wiki/Featured_Artists) beatmaps are more frequently created and ranked. `#mappers-guild` channel is created in the [osu!dev Discord server](/wiki/osu!dev_Discord_server).
- **2019-03-13:** Rule requiring consecutive nominations to be set at least 24 hours apart from each other is removed. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/posts/6885033 -->

### May

- **2019-05-05:** The BN/QAT restructure is finalized. The Quality Assurance Team becomes the [Nomination Assessment Team](/wiki/People/The_Team/Nomination_Assessment_Team), while the Beatmap Nominators become responsible for disqualifications and veto mediation. The [Beatmap Nominator website](https://bn.mappersguild.com/) was published to automate many BN/NAT tasks.

### August

- **2019-08-05:** [Naxess](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/8129817) releases the Mapset Verifier <!-- TODO: stub --> tool, automating a significant portion of beatmap checking processes for Beatmap Nominators.
- **2019-08-06:** Beatmap Nominators are able to participate in BN application evaluations. Their input is used to determine applicants and to judge potential new NAT additions.

### October

- **2018-10-22:** [Ascendance](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2931883) becomes a NAT leader after [Mao](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2204515) steps down.

**3217 beatmaps** were ranked in 2019.

## 2020

### February

- **2020-02-07:** The first Elite Nominator profile badges are awarded to recognize continued exceptional performance from members of the BN team. <!-- https://osu.ppy.sh/home/news/2020-02-07-community-contributors-2019 -->
- **2020-02-21:** [Ascendance](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/2931883) steps down as NAT leader. Most roles of NAT leaders are obsolete by this time.
- **2020-02-24:** The [Quality Assurance Helper](/wiki/People/The_Team/Beatmap_Nominators/General_Information#quality-assurance-helpers) system is transfered from Trello to the [Beatmap Nominator website](https://bn.mappersguild.com/).

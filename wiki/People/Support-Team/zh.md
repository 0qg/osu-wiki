用户组 **Support Team** (又称 **Support Team Redux**，为了不和账号的支持团队的概念混淆，我们一般称之为 "Support Team") 是 osu!Staff 成员，主要管理以下几个论坛版块：<span class="plainlinks">[Development](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/2)</span>, <span class="plainlinks">[Gameplay & Rankings](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/13)</span> (除了 <span class="plainlinks">[Tournaments](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/55)</span> 和 <span class="plainlinks">[Mapping Techniques](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/61)</span> ), <span class="plainlinks">[Skinning](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/15)</span>, <span class="plainlinks">[Feature Requests](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/4)</span> 和 <span class="plainlinks">[Technical Support](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/5)</span>.

职责
====

Support Team 主要负责:

1.  测试并且确认漏洞报告，通知开发者以解决问题。
2.  管理 <span class="plainlinks">[Feature Requests](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/4)</span> 和 <span class="plainlinks">[Technical Support](https://osu.ppy.sh/forum/5)</span> 的帖子，并且用 Resolved, Confirmed, Invalid, Duplicate and Added 标注。

如果有人在这些板块中需要帮助，Support Team 成员可以提供第一手帮助。

# Project Loved Team

The **Project Loved Team** are members of the osu! community who manage [Project Loved](/wiki/Project_Loved), the voting platform that promotes beatmaps to the [Loved](/wiki/Beatmaps#loved) category.

## Roles and Responsibilities

Most of the Project Loved Team consists of **captains** for each of the [Game Modes](/wiki/Game_Modes). Captains are responsible for nominating beatmaps for the Loved category. They also write comments about the nominated maps to help readers get a feel for each map in case they haven't seen or played them before. New nominations are made by the captains roughly every two weeks.

The rest of the team helps with quality control, maintanence and organisation.

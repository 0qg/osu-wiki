# osu!taiko

There are the five difficulties a [beatmapper](/wiki/beatmapper) can [map](/wiki/map) for an osu!taiko [mapset](/wiki/mapset).
The articles below explains how to set your beatmap up for a specific difficulty and what you should be aware of when making them.

- ![Easy icon](/wiki/shared/Easy-t.png) [Futsuu](/wiki/Futsuu)
- ![Normal icon](/wiki/shared/Normal-t.png) [Kantan](/wiki/Kantan)
- ![Hard icon](/wiki/shared/Hard-t.png) [Muzukashii](/wiki/Muzukashii)
- ![Insane icon](/wiki/shared/Insane-t.png) [Oni](/wiki/Oni)
- ![Expert icon](/wiki/shared/Expert-t.png) [Ura Oni](/wiki/Ura_Oni) (also known as _Inner Oni_)

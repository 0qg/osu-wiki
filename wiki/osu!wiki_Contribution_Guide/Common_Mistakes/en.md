## All of my commits say "Updated en.md"

Or with other locale files. This can also be phrased as, "Someone told me to use better commit names."

This means you didn't enter in good commit names when you were able/suppose to. Remeber, commit names are supposed to be meaningful so anyone can understand what you changed without looking at the GitHub diff themselves but short enough to fit the character limit.

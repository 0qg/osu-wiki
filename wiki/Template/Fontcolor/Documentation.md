Usage
-----

**{{Fontcolor|&lt;text color&gt;|&lt;background color&gt;|&lt;text&gt;}}** (remove <background color> when not in use)

Examples of use:

-   **{{Fontcolor|blue|this is about plants}}**: :(it appears in blue text).
-   **{{Fontcolor|\#0033CC|this is about plants}}**: : ([Hex triplet](wikipedia:Web_colors#Hex_triplet "wikilink") codes work also)
-   **{{Fontcolor|red|yellow|red text with yellow background}}**:
-   **{{Fontcolor||red|text in red background}}**: :(note the empty <text color> field "||")

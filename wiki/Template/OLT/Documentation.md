**Important: Do not translate this template! See "Usage" instead.**

A version of to use in other templates (instead of all these "For the YY version of the template, see Template:XX/YY"), or any pages that have language code in the end of their title, separated by a slash (/).

Usage
-----

Add to the top of desired template: `<noinclude>{{OLT}}</noinclude>`. It also works in already translated templates, linking to every other translation of the template, except the original one (English).

To translate "Other languages of the template", use: `<noinclude>{{OLT|Your` `translation}}</noinclude>`

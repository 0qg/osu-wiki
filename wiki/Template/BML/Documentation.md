Usage
-----

    {{BML|1|2}}

-   *parameter 1* = Beatmap number (Please state "s/" or "b/" first)
-   *parameter 2* = Name of beatmap

Example
-------

### s/ \[General beatmap\]

Code: `{{BML|s/51515|Duca` `-` `Clover` `(mapped` `by` `Cilvery)}}`
Result:
Source: [Manually search your own osu! Songs list](Media:sBML.jpg "wikilink") / [Use osu!website beatmap list searching.](http://osu.ppy.sh/p/beatmaplist)

### b/ \[Specific difficulty in beatmap\]

Code: `{{BML|b/157857|Duca` `-` `Clover` `[Easy]` `(mapped` `by` `Cilvery)}}`
Result:
Source: Changing the difficulty tab / the game's "1. Beatmap Listing/Scores"

Note
----

-   s/ = Beatmap in general.
-   b/ = Show that one specific beatmap difficulty in the beatmap.
